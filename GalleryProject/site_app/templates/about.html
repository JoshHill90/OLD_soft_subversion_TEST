About me

Hello there! I'm Carly, a photographer now based in the vibrant city of Nashville, TN. Originally from the beautiful Pacific Northwest, my journey in visual arts began in eastern Washington, sparking a deep love for creative expression. While my background initially led me to a career in film production, my post-graduation exploration brought me to a profound appreciation for capturing moments through the stillness of a camera lens.

With over six years of experience in photography, I've developed a particular fondness for film photography, drawn to its ability to infuse each image with a unique texture and depth. My mission is to craft bold, dramatic scenes saturated with vibrant colors, transporting viewers to other times and places. As a dreamer at heart, I thrive on exploring new concepts and ideas, welcoming a diverse range of projects, from branding and portraits to couples, concerts, and special events.

I would be honored to be a part of capturing your memories and bringing your vision to life in my new home of Nashville, TN. Let's collaborate and create something beautiful together!
