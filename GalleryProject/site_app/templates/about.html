About me

Hi there! I'm Carly, a photographer based in the beautiful Pacific Northwest. Growing up in eastern Washington sparked my love for visual arts and eventually led me to pursue a career in film production. Videography is a passion of mine that I hold near and dear, but after graduating, I fell in love with capturing the stillness through the lens of a camera. I've been drawn to photography for the past six years, and I especially love film photography for its ability to add a unique texture and depth to each image.

My goal is to create bold, dramatic scenes filled with vibrant colors that transport you to another time and place. I'm a dreamer at heart and love to explore new concepts and ideas. I'm open to all types of projects, from branding to portraits, couples to concerts, and special events. I'd be honored to capture your memories and bring your vision to life. Let's create something beautiful together!
